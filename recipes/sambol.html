A fiery and fresh coconut relish made with grated coconut, chili, lime, and onion. Perfect with rice, hoppers, or bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup freshly grated coconut
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 - 2 green or dried red chilies
  • 1 tbsp chili flakes
  • Juice of Â½ lime
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  • Grind coconut, onion, and chilies together using a mortar or food processor.
  • Add chili flakes, lime juice, and salt. Mix thoroughly.
  • Serve fresh.
