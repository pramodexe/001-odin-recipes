Ingredients

  • 2-3 godamba rotis (shredded)
  • 1 cup cooked chicken (shredded or cubed)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 carrot (julienned)
  • 1 cup cabbage (shredded)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  • Heat oil in a large wok or flat griddle.
  • SautÃ© garlic, onions, and vegetables until softened.
  • Add chicken and curry powder. Stir well.
  • Push ingredients to the side and scramble eggs.
  • Mix everything together, then add shredded roti.
  • Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  • Stir-fry until all flavors are well combined and heated.
