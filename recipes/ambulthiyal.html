üêü Ambul Thiyal (Sour Fish Curry)

A traditional southern dish with chunks of fish simmered in a tangy, peppery, and spiced goraka-based paste.

Ingredients

  • 500g firm fish (tuna is best)
  • 5 - 6 pieces dried goraka (soaked and ground)
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 2 cloves garlic (sliced)
  • 1 inch ginger (sliced)
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  • Mix goraka paste, spices, and salt into a thick marinade.
  • Coat the fish pieces and let marinate 30 mins.
  • Place in a clay pot or pan with curry leaves and a little water.
  • Cook on low heat until water evaporates and oil separates.
