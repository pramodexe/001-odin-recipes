üç™ Watalappan (Sri Lankan Spiced Coconut Custard)

A rich, creamy dessert made from jaggery, coconut milk, and eggs, flavored with cardamom and nutmeg.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup grated jaggery
  • 1 cup thick coconut milk
  • 4 eggs
  • ¬Ω tsp cardamom powder
  • Pinch of grated nutmeg
  • 1 tsp vanilla (optional)
  • Cashews for garnish

Instructions

  • Melt jaggery with a little coconut milk. Let cool.
  • Beat eggs lightly and mix with cooled jaggery.
  • Add remaining coconut milk, spices, and vanilla.
  • Pour into ramekins or dish, top with cashews.
  • Steam for 30–40 minutes until set. Chill before serving.
